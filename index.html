About Me

Hi, I’m Bhavy Chavda, a passionate Full Stack Web Developer with a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies. Over the past year, I’ve honed my skills in WordPress development, custom plugin creation, and responsive web design. My expertise includes working with JavaScript, PHP, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, AJAX, and jQuery to build dynamic, user-friendly websites. I thrive on solving complex problems and creating seamless digital experiences that are both functional and visually appealing.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Application and am currently pursuing a Master’s in Computer Science, specializing in CA & IT. My academic journey has been marked by consistent high performance, earning me academic rankings throughout my BCA program. I’ve also worked on several projects, such as an online outdoor advertisement booking system and a construction service booking platform, which have further strengthened my technical and problem-solving skills.

What drives me is the opportunity to collaborate with teams and leverage my technical expertise to bring innovative ideas to life. Whether it’s developing custom Elementor widgets, optimizing database management with MySQL, or ensuring responsive design across devices, I’m committed to delivering high-quality solutions. I’m excited to continue growing as a developer and contributing to impactful projects that make a difference.
